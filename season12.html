South Park

Season 12

Season 12 was a bright time for South Park, as Matt Stone and Trey Parker released some of their most infamous episodes. From Canada going on strike to the dangers of cheesing and adventuring for available Wi-Fi, season 12 has a lot to offer to fans and viewers alike. While it isn't as memorable to me as season 8 and season 5, I still enjoyed this season a lot and the positives really outweigh the bad episodes that stick out like sore thumbs. There are some funny and satisfying moments throughout, like Wendy beating Cartman for making fun of breast cancer or the town of South Park being under attack by giant hampsters. Overall, season 12 is a high tier season for me.
